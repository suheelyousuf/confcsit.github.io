About The Conference

The main objective of this National Conference on “Computational Methods, Data Science and Applications” is to gauge the emerging trends in computational methods, data sciences, machine learning and their real-time applications and capture and explain these patterns in their research.

The One-Day National Conference will act as a platform for scholars, researchers and students to present and share their work and findings. It will further allow them to situate their research in the broader field of study of applications of Data Science, machine learning to solve real-world problems. It would also allow them to identify potential areas of further explorations.
